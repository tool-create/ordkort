About Ordkort

Ordkort is a free Danish vocabulary flashcard app for people learning Danish as a second language.

It contains over 1,785 curated Danish words with English translations, word types, example sentences, and module levels aligned with Danskuddannelse 3.

  • Danish ↔ English modes
  • Typed and voice input
  • Progress tracking and day streaks
  • Spaced repetition review
  • Module filter (Modul 1–5)
  • Word type filter
  • Upload your own vocabulary deck (xlsx)
