About Ordkort

Ordkort is a free Danish vocabulary flashcard app built for people learning Danish as a second language.

It contains over 1,700 curated Danish words with English translations, word types, example sentences, and module levels aligned with the Danish language education system (Danskuddannelse 3).

Features

  • Danish → English and English → Danish modes
  • Typed and voice input
  • Progress tracking and day streaks
  • Module filter (Modul 1–5)
  • Word type filter
  • Example sentences for most words
  • Upload your own vocabulary deck (xlsx)

Free forever

Ordkort is free to use. No sign-up, no tracking, no subscription. All data stays in your browser.
